Gifts

A birthday gift is a present given to someone to celebrate their birthday. It is a thoughtful gesture intended to express love, appreciation, or friendship on the occasion of the person's birth. Birthday gifts come in various forms and can range from tangible items like toys, books, clothes, or gadgets to experiences such as tickets to a concert, a spa day, or a special outing. The choice of a birthday gift often reflects the giver's knowledge of the recipient's interests, preferences, and desires. The act of giving a birthday gift is a common tradition in many cultures and is a way to make the birthday person feel special and valued.

Versatile DIY Pop-up Card

A popup card is a creative and visually engaging type of greeting card that features three-dimensional elements that "pop up" when the card is opened. These cards are a delightful way to add a touch of surprise and personalization to your greetings for various occasions, such as birthdays, holidays, or special milestones. While there are many intricate popup card designs available, making a simple popup card is a great starting point for beginners.

Materials Needed:
1. Colored cardstock or construction paper (two contrasting colors)
2. Scissors
3. Pencil
4. Ruler
5. Glue or double-sided tape
6. Decorative elements (stickers, markers, etc.)

Paper Gift Box

A paper gift bag is a decorative and functional packaging solution for presenting gifts. It serves as an alternative to traditional wrapping paper and provides a convenient way to carry and present a gift in an aesthetically pleasing manner. These bags are often made from sturdy, decorative paper and can be customized to suit the occasion, making them a versatile and eco-friendly option for gift-giving.

Materials:
1. Coloured A4 paper or wrapping paper 2. Glue 3. Ribbon

Chocolate Bouquet

A chocolate bouquet is a delightful and visually appealing arrangement of chocolates or candies presented in a way that resembles a floral bouquet. It makes for a charming and edible gift, suitable for various occasions such as birthdays, anniversaries, Valentine's Day, or as a sweet gesture to express appreciation or love. Creating a chocolate bouquet involves arranging individual chocolates on sticks or skewers, often resembling the stems of flowers, and presenting them in a decorative manner.

Materials:
1. Coloured A4 paper / wrapping paper
2. Scissors
3. Scotch Tape
4. Wooden stick
5. Ribbon
6. Chocolate

Popsicle Stick Photo Frames

It's a simple and creative DIY project where popsicle sticks are used to create a frame for holding and displaying photos. This craft involves arranging and gluing popsicle sticks together to form a rectangular or square shape, which serves as the border for a photo. Once assembled, the frame can be decorated with paint, markers, or other embellishments to add a personalized touch. It's a popular and budget-friendly way to showcase memories and photos in a charming and handmade manner.

Materials:
1. Popsicle sticks
2. Glue (UHU glue)
3. Photos
4. Decorative Elements (optional)
5. Paintbrushes (if using paint)
